## Empatixtech Takes Data Privacy Seriously

### What is the GDPR?

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) has been in effect since 25 May 2018. It applies uniformly across all EU and EEA countries.

The goals of the GDPR are to:

- Harmonize data protection laws across the EU;
- Modernize regulations in light of technological advancements;
- Strengthen individual rights over personal data;
- Increase accountability for data controllers and processors;
- Promote transparency in how, by whom, and for what purposes data is processed.

The GDPR applies to any organization processing the personal data of individuals within the EU, regardless of the company’s location. This includes data related to Empatixtech’s clients, end users, and employees.

### What is Empatixtech doing about the GDPR?

Empatixtech acts as both a data controller and processor and is committed to GDPR compliance. We have undertaken a thorough program to align our practices with GDPR requirements, including:

- Conducting a global audit of our data processing activities;
- Reviewing and strengthening data security across all business units;
- Updating internal policies, staff training, and data governance procedures;
- Establishing GDPR-compliant agreements with all data processors and sub-processors;
- Reviewing our use of cookies and tracking technologies on websites and in marketing emails;
- Improving user consent and marketing preference management.

### Empatixtech’s commitments to clients and data subjects

Empatixtech is committed to processing personal data responsibly and in full alignment with GDPR. This includes:

- Processing personal data only on documented instructions, as per our contract with you or as required by law;
- Implementing suitable technical and organizational safeguards, such as endpoint protection, encryption, secure remote access, and access control policies;
- Ensuring that only authorized personnel under confidentiality obligations can access personal data;
- Engaging sub-processors only with your consent and ensuring they follow the same data protection standards as Empatixtech;
- Alerting you if we believe your instructions may lead to a GDPR violation;
- Assisting you in responding to data subject requests and inquiries from regulatory authorities;
- Promptly notifying you of any data breaches or suspected breaches that could compromise your information;
- Ensuring appropriate legal safeguards for any transfers of personal data outside the EU/EEA.

This list is not exhaustive. Empatixtech is dedicated to fulfilling all GDPR obligations related to the data we process on behalf of our clients and their end users.
