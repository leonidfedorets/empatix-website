### 2\. Performance Cookies

These Cookies gather data about how visitors use Empatixtech Sites. This includes details such as browser type, operating system, referring websites, visit frequency, visit duration, and pages viewed. The data collected is aggregated and does not personally identify you. We use this information to enhance user experience and improve site performance.

### 3\. Functionality Cookies

These Cookies remember your preferences and choices (such as username, language, or region) to provide a more personalized experience. They can also be used to remember layout changes or enable services like video playback or commenting. These Cookies may be anonymized and do not track your activity across other websites.
